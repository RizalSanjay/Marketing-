Course Description

This course on “Marketing” provides a comprehensive introduction to essential marketing concepts and strategies. Learners will explore the fundamentals of marketing, including its importance in driving business growth and customer engagement...

Target Audience

  • Marketing Professionals
  • Business Owners and Entrepreneurs
  • Students and Graduates
  • Aspiring Marketers
  • Freelancers and Consultants

Learning Objectives

  1. Understand and explain the core concepts, importance, and evolution of marketing in business success.
  2. Apply market segmentation techniques to effectively target specific consumer groups.
  3. Develop and implement positioning strategies to differentiate products in a competitive market.
  4. Analyze the role of branding and packaging in influencing customer perception and loyalty.
  5. Evaluate and select the most efficient distribution channels for delivering products to customers.

Course Structure

  • Module 1: Introduction to Marketing
  • Module 2: History and Development of Marketing
  • Module 3: Understanding Market Segmentation
  • Module 4: Effective Positioning Strategies
  • Module 5: Channels of Distribution
